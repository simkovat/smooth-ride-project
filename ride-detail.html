Where is your next longboard ride gonna take you?

Prokopské údolí

Super chilled ride through this valley will make you forget that you are in a capital. The road is slightly downhill most of the time, so you're not gonna sweat too much. You can stop for a drink at the pub U Prokopa. At the end, it is possible to cross the Vltava river on a ferry and continue on another trail.

Access

START: Metro B, Nove Butovice station, walk 10 minutes to the starting point
END: Lihovar - 10 minutes to the city center by tram, bus, ferry

Other tips

Best time for a ride is morning or evening and weekdays. We do not recommend going during the weekend as it gets crowded with pedestrians, bikers and dogs ;)
